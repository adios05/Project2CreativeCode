Outfit Generator

By Adi Suresh

Welcome to the dress-up game! You are a fashion designer set to showcase your newest look on the catwalk. Use the arrow keys to create your outfit! The right arrow key will switch between different top options, and the left will switch between different skirts! When you have a combo you love, press "Create Outfit" and the game will give you a mock-up of a brand new outfit based on your design. Change the color of the outfit with the "Change Color" button! When you are happy with your final outfit, press "Save Outfit" to save a PNG of your outfit on the stage to your device! Wanna play again? Just press "Try Again".
